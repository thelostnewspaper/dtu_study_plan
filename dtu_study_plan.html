Semester 4 — Spring February – June 30 ECTS

Master's Thesis — 30 ECTS

The thesis is your single most important career asset. An industry-partnered thesis is what converts the degree into a job offer for international graduates.

Start researching company partners in Semester 2. DTU career office and department boards post live company collaboration opportunities.
Contact potential supervisors by October of Semester 3. Supervisors fill up early.
Target companies: Vestas, Grundfos, Terma, Ericsson Denmark, Siemens Gamesa, Kamstrup, Danfoss, MAN Energy — all have English-first engineering cultures and DTU pipelines.
Suggested angle: "Security architecture for real-time networked embedded systems under NIS2 compliance constraints" — or any OT/ICS security topic relevant to your partner company.
Notify the head of study you want "Embedded and Distributed Systems" on your diploma before submitting the thesis.
